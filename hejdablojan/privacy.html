Privacy Policy

Bye Bye Diaper (Hejdå blöjan) — last updated: 2026-07-01

Summary

Bye Bye Diaper collects no personal information whatsoever. The app works fully offline. Nothing you enter ever leaves your device, and there are no accounts, servers, analytics, or ads.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data about you or your child.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — your child's profile, potty-training progress, logged events, and reminder settings — is stored only locally on your device. It is never uploaded to any server or cloud service. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

No accounts, no network

The app requires no sign-up or login and does not need an internet connection to work. It makes no network requests that carry personal data.

No tracking, analytics, or advertising

The app contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party marketing SDKs. No usage profiles are created. There is no Firebase or comparable service.

Children's privacy

Bye Bye Diaper is a guide for parents. Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no information from children either. It contains no ads and no external links aimed at children.
